Frequently Asked Questions

What is Presend?

Presend is the new online gift-shopping experience. You can now send gifts to your friends while having the convenience of online shopping. There won’t be any more last-minute shopping, or sore legs. Above all, our exquisite selection of gifts are meant to delight yourself and your friends.

How do I send a gift?

After selecting a gift from our catalogue, you can send it by inputting your friend’s email address or mobile number at checkout.

How do I accept a gift?

A link will be sent to the recipient via email or SMS. After accessing the link, the recipient can then accept the gift by inputting their preferred shipping address. Otherwise, they will be able to select another gift of the same or lower value.

When will the gift be shipped and when will it arrive?

The gift will be shipped after the recipient enters their shipping address. It will arrive within 28 days upon confirmation.

When is Presend launching?

Presend will launch in June 2020. Stay tuned and sign up for the latest news and promotion!
